Descendants of Margaret THOMAS

First Generation

1. Margaret THOMAS was born 1 May 1860 in Derby, Derbyshire. She died 22 Jul 1931 in Dewsbury, York and was buried 25 Jul 1931 in South Ossett Parish Church Grave ref No NG E6..

Margaret married (1) Thomas IMESON, son of Philip IMESON and Hannah CREASER, on 17 Feb 1878 in Dewsbury, York. Thomas was born 6 Nov 1856 in Great Driffield, York. He died 5 Nov 1881 in Earlsheaton, York.

They had the following children:

+ 2 M i Philip IMESON was born 8 Feb 1879 and died Jan 1943.
+ 3 F ii Mary Emily IMESON was born 24 May 1881 and died 24 Nov 1955.

Margaret also married (2) Charles FLOWERS, son of George FLOWERS and Mary Ann FLOWERS, on 12 Jan 1884 in Dewsbury, York. Charles was born 23 Mar 1854 in Wakefield, York. He died 1 Feb 1896 in Thornhill Lees, York.

They had the following children:

+ 4 M iii George FLOWERS was born 26 Dec 1886.
+ 5 M iv William FLOWERS was born 11 Jan 1889 and died 18 Aug 1959.
+ 6 M v Joseph FLOWERS was born 5 May 1891.
+ 7 F vi Lily FLOWERS was born 6 Sep 1893 and died 1950.

Margaret also married (3) William CARRUTHERS, son of George CARRUTHERS and Ann PHILLISKIRK, on 29 Sep 1900 in Dewsbury, York. William was born about 1855 in Hartlepool, Durham. He died 31 Mar 1913 in Thornhill Lees, York.

They had the following children:
